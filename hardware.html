Nunchuck Controller:



Nunchuck Controller is a homemade wii nunchuck midi adapter, that converts sensor data from wii nunchucks (joystick, 2 buttons, and accelerometer(motion sensor)) into Midi CC messages that can be directly mapped to parameters in any music software.
